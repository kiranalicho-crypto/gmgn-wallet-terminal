Critical correction
-------------------
The Birdeye raw response states meta.holding_check=false. The prior comparison
used the returned holding value in a quantity reconciliation formula. Therefore
the count of 26 "transfer or missing history" flags is not reliable and must not
be used. The V2 bulk prefilter does not use holding, current value, or unrealized
PnL to infer transfers.

Decision
--------
Use Birdeye on all 83 corrected provisional wallets as a fast candidate
generator with a $30K safety threshold. Do not declare final Y success or
failure from Birdeye alone. Any candidate must be checked with a transfer-aware,
transaction-ordered, on-chain FIFO ledger.
